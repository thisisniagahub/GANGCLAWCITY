⚖️ Wireframe #3: Problem/Solution Split

Concept: Split-screen showing chaos vs. clarity

💡 Core Idea:

Left side shows the chaos of current AI tools (10 tabs, dashboards, errors). Right side shows GANGCLAWCITY's organized city. User can toggle between views or see both side-by-side. Visual contrast > words.

    Zero Visibility Your AI agents run in the background. No idea what they're doing. Hope for the best.
  • 📑
    10 Browser Tabs One tab for chat, one for automation, one for analytics. Context switching hell.
  • ❌
    Error Messages "Rate limit exceeded", "API key invalid", "Task failed". Debugging at 2 AM.
  • 💸
    No ROI Tracking Spending $200/month on AI tools. No idea what you're getting back.

  • ✅
