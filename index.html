What this project is

One operating story for AI work, control, and scale.

The product direction is simple: stop presenting disconnected demos and start shipping a single GANGCLAWCITY platform with a strong operator surface, a memorable city metaphor, and a real path to production hardening.

Instead of building everything from zero, GANGCLAWCITY takes successful foundations, removes vendor framing, aligns the architecture, and improves the system until it feels like one serious company product.
